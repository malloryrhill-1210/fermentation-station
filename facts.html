Facts About Kombucha

What is Kombucha

Kombucha is a fermented tea. The basic ingredients of Kombucha are yeast, sugar, and black tea. The process of fermentation creates bacteria and acids that form a film on top of the liquid called a SCOBY (symbiotic colony of bacteria and yeast).
